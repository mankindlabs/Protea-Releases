## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take security seriously at Protea Browser. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly.

### How to Report

1. **Email**: security@proteabrowser.com
2. **Subject**: `[SECURITY] Brief description`
3. **Include**:
   - Description of the vulnerability
   - Steps to reproduce
   - Potential impact
   - Any suggested fixes (optional)

### Response Timeline

- **Acknowledgment**: Within 48 hours
- **Initial Assessment**: Within 7 days
- **Resolution Target**: Within 30 days for critical issues

### What to Expect

- You will receive confirmation of your report
- We will keep you updated on our progress
- Credit will be given in release notes (unless you prefer anonymity)
- We will not take legal action against good-faith security researchers
